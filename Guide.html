Travel Tips for Visitors

  • 🧳 Best travel seasons: Spring (Mar–May) & Autumn (Sep–Nov)
  • 🏔️ Carry warm clothes for mountain regions
  • 🚐 Use local guides or registered travel agencies
  • 📱 NTC/Ncell SIM cards for easy communication
  • 💰 Local currency: Nepalese Rupees (NPR)
  • 🙏 Respect local traditions and temples

How to Reach Major Destinations

Most provinces are connected by highways and domestic flights. Tribhuvan International Airport (Kathmandu) is the main entry point for foreign visitors.
